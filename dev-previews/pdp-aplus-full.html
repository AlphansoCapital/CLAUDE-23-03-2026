How It Works

The 4-phase Glow-Lock™ delivery system

01
Absorption (0–5 mins)
The lightweight gel formula breaks down on skin contact, releasing low-molecular Hyaluronic Acid that immediately begins drawing moisture from the atmosphere into the upper skin layers. Skin feels instantly hydrated without the heavy or greasy residue of oil-based creams.
💡 Apply on slightly damp skin for maximum HA absorption
02
Penetration (5–30 mins)
The Vitamin E and peptide complex begin penetrating the dermal-epidermal junction. Peptide signalling molecules bind to collagen receptor sites, triggering the skin's natural repair mechanisms. This is when the structural anti-ageing work begins.
💡 Avoid washing face for at least 30 mins after application
03
Barrier Lock (30 mins–8 hrs)
Vitamin E forms a protective lipid layer over the skin, sealing in all active ingredients and preventing trans-epidermal water loss (TEWL). This barrier also protects against free radical damage from UV, pollution, and blue light throughout the day.
💡 Apply SPF on top in the morning — Vit E boosts SPF effectiveness
04
Overnight Repair (8–48 hrs)
Kashmiri Saffron and Himalayan Rose actives continue working during the skin's overnight repair cycle, reducing melanin production at the cellular level. With consistent twice-daily use, cumulative brightening is visible from day 10–14.
💡 Results accelerate significantly after 4 weeks of consistent use
