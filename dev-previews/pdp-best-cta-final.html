The Science

How It Actually Works

Most moisturisers sit on top of the skin. Urban Trend's Glow-Lock™ delivery system works in 4 phases — from the surface down to the dermal layer — so every active ingredient reaches where it needs to be.

01
Absorption  (0–5 min)
Lightweight gel breaks down on contact, releasing low-molecular HA that draws moisture from the atmosphere directly into the upper skin layers. Skin feels instantly hydrated with zero greasiness.
💡 Apply on slightly damp skin for 2× absorption
02
Penetration  (5–30 min)
Vitamin E and Matrixyl 3000 peptides penetrate the dermal-epidermal junction. Peptide signalling molecules bind to collagen receptors and trigger the skin's natural repair cycle.
💡 Avoid washing face for 30 mins after application
03
Barrier Lock  (30 min–8 hrs)
Vitamin E forms a protective lipid layer over skin, sealing all actives and preventing trans-epidermal water loss. Shields against UV, pollution, and blue light throughout the day.
💡 Vitamin E boosts your SPF's effectiveness when layered
04
Overnight Repair  (8–48 hrs)
Kashmiri Saffron and Himalayan Rose work during the skin's overnight repair cycle, reducing melanin at the cellular level. Cumulative brightening from day 10–14 with consistent use.
💡 Results accelerate significantly after 4 weeks
